TECHNOLOGY

    To bring content to variety of touchscreen devices we mainly use HTML5 technology. It allows us to take full advantage of the richer and more flexible design, bringing a more engaging reader experience compatible across different platforms.

Some advantages of HTML5 are:

  • Quick production of digital copy
  • Flexibility to use different platforms. Once digital copy is built for one platform it is quick to make copy for another platform.
  • Easy to edit content even after publishing
  • HTML5 is less expensive development solution compare to native OS applications
  • Final reader doesn't feel any difference between HTML5 app and native OS application.

    In case when we are not able to use HTML5 we are free to develop native application for every platform.
