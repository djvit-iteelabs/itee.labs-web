Post #1 - First day alive

April 17th, 2011 by Alex Kornilov

Facebook | Twitter

Hi everyone,
We are very excited to open our website. As a team we've being together for half a year, but it took us some time to bring our presence to web.

We strongly believe in changes in WWW. It is obvious that web 2.0 is quickly transforming into web 3.0. This transformation boosts fast development of mobile devices. Internet moves from PC to phones, tablets and other gadgets. And that means a lot for publishing industry. New ideas, new possibilities, new opportunities are open for everyone who is in love with "words" and "pages". And we are happy to help publishers, writers and companies to bring their books, magazines, newspapers and booklets to verity of mobile devices.

Actually, we are not company. Yeah, officially we are, but I prefer to say that we are project team. We have different specialist and practitioners in our team to satisfy project requirements. Everyone can do his part of project from any place in the World. Using online project management tools and cloud services, we've built network between members in a way we could efficiently collaborate between each other during work on the project. Also we can quickly find needed people and involve them into the work. By organizing our work in such way we are able to illuminate unneeded cost like office overheads, hardware purchasing and others. So, our main assets are people's brains. And that structure makes us light, quick, thinkable and effective.

We like to create new things. Revolution in mobile digital publishing gives us huge opportunities to develop tones of new cool stuff. It is amazing when you see how imagination becomes reality and you involved in this process.

Providing right solutions to our partners makes us happy and satisfied. It makes us believe that we add value to product and service. And general idea is very simple - combine best of the web and best of the printed issue.

First step is done. We are open and looking forward to put our hands on new interesting projects and meet interesting people. We will try to tweet frequently, write constantly and work hard.

Alex
