About
 

itee.labs is a digital publisher, working with companies mainly to provide solutions for bringing content to variety of touchscreen devices.

Our main area of expertise:



It is hard to imagine business which does not use printing materials like booklets, brochures, leaflets and flyers. And today companies are looking for ways to deliver information to customers using expanding array of mobile devices.

Also, it is important to make it fast and effective, keeping reading experience in a wayperson can read all content page by page.

Enjoy books on their chosen device anywhere and anytime without having to deal with incompatible libraries - this is what readers waiting for. From other hand, publishers look for cost-effective authoring workflows, copyright protection, and distribution management for e-books.

Digital book publishing allows reach readers worldwide in new way within the moment.

Due to customer desire to shop when, where, and how they want, retailers are looking for approach to combine e-commerce with print catalog. In order to provide valuable shopping experience, it is really important to combine convenience of online store browsing with natural flipping of print catalog.

With itee.labs solutions publishers can extend a magazine experience in a touchlayout and find compromise between design commitment and information susceptibility.

Moreover, they can focus on producing paper versions and itee.labs will take careabout delivering rich-media copy in multiple screen formats.
