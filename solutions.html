SOLUTIONS FOR DIGITAL PUBLISHING

The Challenge

The Digital Age for the publishing industry has already begun. This revolution has expanded the way readers consume content, eliminating borders between magazine, shop, game, and movie. People are seeking fascinating rich media experiences wherever they go, using their preferred devices. In today's world, in order to build distinctive brands, develop sustainable business strategies, and achieve greater profitability, companies need to deliver optimized content to a diverse audience on an expanding array of smartphones, tablets, e-readers, and other gadgets.

  • PERIODICALS
  • BOOKS
  • BOOKLETS
  • CATALOGS

 

The Solution
Our solutions allow publishers to efficiently create, realize, and distribute ground-breaking content to the broadest possible audience on a variety of digital devices and platforms. itee.labs is helping publishers and advertisers revolutionize the way they design and deliver digital content.

Advantages
- rich media capability (pictures, photo, music, video, 360° product view, etc.)
- no additional distribution cost
- worldwide audience targeting
- more engaging ads
- truly reading, not simply browsing
- easy selection of target audience
- capacity for online shops
- green publishing
- instant content and opinion sharing

Richness of digital media
        itee.labs' work allows publishers and advertisers to create an impressive reading experience that links the experience of print design, the advantage of the tablet format, the engagement of touch interface, the enjoyment of rich media (such as video, audio, animation, 360° view) and more.

Advertising revenue
        Digital publishing provides a sensational new form for product advertisement. Readers will soon be able to interact with attractive, immersive ads alongside other magazine content. This will allow boosts in ad revenue and increased profitability.
