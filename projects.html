"KLIK VOOR SOEDAN" CHARITY WEBSITE

 


Client
Edet
www.iteelabs.com/edet/

 06.11.2010


Watch a short animation about how Edet is helping children

 

The Task
Create social good webpage for charity campaign in Sudan.

The Brief
Edet is a company based in Netherlands which produces Personal Hygiene products and distribute it all over the World. Their tissue products are important for personal hygiene and provide comfort in daily lives.
        Also, Edet is concern about people around the Globe. They always take part in social initiatives and make donations in order to make our planet more comfortable for living.
        For charity campaign in Sudan, Edet needed a webpage which would help them to build toilets for pupils in schools. All visitors, who will click on a project, will help Edet to bring good to children. Every time there will be 100 000 clicks, new toilet would be built near the school.

"INFOSYSTEM"
CITY INFORMATION SYSTEM

 


The Task
Design and create touchscreen optimized information system for screens in Altstatten, Switzerland.

The Brief
Swiss Screen is a company in Switzerland which offers integrated communication solutions for individual requirements. It has a number of information screens all over the city which translate news and info about Altstatten.
        In order to provide new level of interactive experience and increase utility for people, company decided to implement touchscreen solution. Now, with just a tap of a finger, users can easily read news, get weather forecast, receive shopping information, find hotel, use maps and many other things.
